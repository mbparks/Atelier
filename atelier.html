Atelier

Field Instrument No. 035, the preparatory bench. Everything worked out before a brush touches the surface, in four families that share one document.

See reduces the picture to value, temperature, or edges, and lifts a color key. Build lays the armature, transfer grid, and perspective. Color holds the harmony wheel, the Gurney gamut mask, and the Kubelka-Munk mixing bench. Check flips, measures, and weighs the design.

The reference never leaves your browser. All processing is local. License GPL-3.0.
